Progression Design

Power Curve & Learning Arc

Extreme Initial Weakness

Starting off extremely weak and dependent on the ridden

Indirect Power Growth

Power scales through the enhancement of the Nervendaggers, the ridden, and the bond between them

Observational Repetition

Observing enemy patterns, understanding traits, mastering timing
The game rewards skill-based learning over grinding

Mastery of Systemic Layers

Optimize dyad, choose mutations wisely, manage consumables efficiently, strategically navigate between exploration, rest, and risk

Structures & Milestones

Narrative-Gated Progression

Each major region locked behind a key boss

World-Integrated Checkpoint Systems

Water Sources are central: they serve as checkpoints, nerve consolidation hubs, and upgrade points

Sacrificial Choice

Transfer to a new ridden or strengthen the current one? Advancement requires meaningful sacrifices

Multiple Vectors of Evolution

Nervendagger level

Unlocks access to stronger riddens

Ridden growth

Via cruspowers, traits, and psychological powers

Consumables

Stat boosters, Antidotes, Food

Equipment

Armor, Weapons, Injections

Bond level

Amplifies psychological power potential
